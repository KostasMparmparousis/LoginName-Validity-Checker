Format Rules.

Usernames recommended from the Proposer service are obliged to follow a specific set of rules that ensure their validity, regarding their character length, the separators used and their syntax.

To elaborate, the following rules are currently in place:

  • Usernames must have a minimum length of 4 characters and maximum length of 20.
  • Available separators are: [ "." ]
  • A username must begin with one or more latin alphanumerical characters ([a-z0-9]).
  • Zero or one instances of a separator can follow the first alphanumerical characters.
  • Usernames must also end with a set of alphanumerical characters.
